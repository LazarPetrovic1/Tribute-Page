! Bill Gates

Bill Gates

The smartest words of the most successful man.

Here is a list of his accomplishments:

  • 1955: Born to William Henry Gates II, an attorney and Mary Gates, a school teacher.
  • 1967: Enrolled in the Lakeside school in Seattle and befriends Paul Allen.
  • 1973: Enters Harvard as a pre-law major, but shifts focus to computer science.
  • 1975: Gates calls MITS. He offers to develop software for the MITS Altair.
  • 1976: Gates and Allen trademark Microsoft.
  • 1977: Takes a second leave from school and sets up Microsoft.
  • 1978: Microsoft’s year-end sales exceed $1 million.
  • 1979: Microsoft headquarters are relocated to Bellevue, Wash.
  • 1983: Allen leaves Microsoft after developing Hodgkin’s disease.
  • 1987: Gates becomes the youngest billionaire, at 31.
  • 1995: Gates becomes the richest man in the world, in July.
  • 1999: Gates publishes "Business @ the Speed of Thought".
  • 2000: Gates steps down as the CEO of Microsoft.
  • 2001: The long anti-trust sage ends for Microsoft.
  • 2005: Queen Elizabeth II knights Bill Gates.
  • 2007: Bill Gates "graduates" from Harvard University.
  • 2008: Gates retires from day-to-day duties, but still retains the role of chairman.
